About Us

Along with the COVID-19 pandemic we are also fighting an `infodemic'. Fake news and rumors are rampant on social media . Such misinformation has caused confusion among people, disruptions in society, and even deadly consequences in health problems.

To help researchers combat COVID-19 health misinformation, we present CoviFacts, the first ever web application to protect the social engagement of fake news among the people.
